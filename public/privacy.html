KidCapital

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

KidCapital ("we", "us", "our") is an educational board game app designed to teach children ages 8–14 about financial literacy. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information within our app.

Summary: KidCapital does not collect personal information from children. The app is designed to be safe, educational, and compliant with children's privacy laws worldwide.

1. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

KidCapital is designed for children and complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy Requirements.

  • We do not collect personal information from children under 13 (or under the applicable age in your jurisdiction).
  • We do not require children to provide any personal information to use the app.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, photos, or any other personally identifiable information from children.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID / AAID) or any device identifiers to track users.
  • We do not collect or transmit device serial numbers, IMEI, MAC addresses, SSID, BSSID, SIM serial numbers, or build serial numbers.
  • We do not request or collect location data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • Game Progress: Player names chosen in-game, game scores, and settings are stored locally on the device. When an optional anonymous account is created for leaderboard features, only a randomly generated anonymous identifier is used — no personal data is linked.
  • No Analytics Tracking: We do not use analytics SDKs that track individual user behavior, browsing habits, or create user profiles.
  • No Advertising: KidCapital does not display any advertisements. We do not use any advertising SDKs or ad networks.

3. Third-Party Services

KidCapital uses a limited number of third-party services, none of which collect personal information from children:

  • Supabase (Game Sync): Used solely for optional anonymous game progress synchronization and leaderboard functionality. Only randomly generated anonymous identifiers are stored — no personal information is collected, transmitted, or linked to children.
  • RevenueCat (Subscription Management): Used to manage optional subscription purchases. Purchases are handled through the device's app store (Google Play / Apple App Store) and require parental action. RevenueCat does not collect personal information from children.

We do not use any SDKs or APIs that are prohibited for use in child-directed apps.

4. Data Protection (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable local laws:

  • We process minimal data (anonymous identifiers only) on the legal basis of legitimate interest for providing the game service.
  • No personal data is collected, so there is no personal data to access, correct, delete, or port.
  • Anonymous game data may be stored on servers located in secure data centers. Since no personal data is involved, no data transfer safeguards are required.

5. Parental Rights

We believe parents and guardians should be in control of their children's digital experience:

  • Since we do not collect personal information from children, there is no personal data to review, delete, or modify.
  • In-app purchases require parental action (app store authentication).
  • Parents can contact us at any time with questions or concerns about their child's privacy.
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected any personal information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. In-App Purchases

KidCapital offers optional subscription plans. All purchases are processed through the device's official app store (Google Play Store or Apple App Store) and are subject to the store's parental controls and purchase authentication requirements. We do not directly process any payment information.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data associated with our app. Since we do not collect personal information, the risk to user privacy is minimal. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (TLS/SSL).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
